Dollywood's Famous Fried Chicken

Description

A scrumptious favorite from Dolly Parton's Dollywood, this delicious dish is sure to please every family. Following a recipe honed over generations, you can enjoy the flavors of the Smokey Mountains in the convenience of your own home.

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs. AP Flour
  • 1‚ÅÑ2 cup Salt
  • 3 T Pepper
  • 3 T Garlic (granulated)
  • 2 T Onion (granulated)
  • 2 T Paprika
  • 1 T Cayenne
  • Chicken Broken down into 8 pieces

Directions

  1. Turn fryer up to 350 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients using a whisk. Rinse chicken in ice cold water, drain and add to the flour mixture.
  3. Stir until chicken is coated completely, then add to the 350 degree fryer.
  4. Cook for 12-15 minutes or until the internal temp of the chicken reaches 165 degrees.
